Burger

A simple burger

A quick recipe for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Oil
  • Buns
  • Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Mix ground beef with salt and pepper
  2. Form patties from ground beef mixture
  3. Preheat pan
  4. Add oil then patties
  5. Add cheese when the patties are cooked
  6. Assemble burgers
